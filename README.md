# 📞 Call Center Performance Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of call center operations between January and March 2021. The dashboard evaluates call volume, agent performance, response efficiency, and customer satisfaction to identify operational gaps and improvement opportunities.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Preview
![Dashboard](https://github.com/gdogara-glitch/Call_center_performance_analysis/blob/main/screenshots/Call_centre%20page%201%20Screenshot.png?raw=true)

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
The objective is to assess call center efficiency by analyzing response rates, resolution performance, and customer satisfaction, in order to improve service delivery and operational effectiveness.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- Total Calls: 5,000  
- Answered Calls: 4,054 (81%)  
- Unanswered Calls: 946 (19%)  
- Resolved Calls: 3,646 (73%)  
- Unresolved Calls: 1,354 (27%)  
- Customer Satisfaction: 69%  
- Average Speed of Answer: 68 seconds (Target: 60 seconds)  
- Total Agents: 8  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📈 Call Volume Trend
- Call volumes fluctuate over time with noticeable peaks and declines.
- A sharp drop towards the end suggests possible external or seasonal factors affecting demand.

### 📉 Unanswered Calls
- Although relatively low, unanswered calls are consistently present.
- Indicates gaps in staffing or call handling efficiency.

### 🧩 Resolution Performance
- Resolution rate of 73% shows most issues are handled effectively.
- However, 27% unresolved cases highlight room for improvement.

### 😊 Customer Satisfaction
- Satisfaction level is moderate at 69%.
- Suggests service delivery is acceptable but not optimal.

### ⏱ Response Time
- Average response time is 68 seconds, above the 60-second target.
- Delay in response may negatively impact customer experience.

### 📌 Topic Analysis
- Major call drivers:
  - Streaming (~20%)
  - Technical Support (~20%)
  - Payment Issues (~20%)
- Indicates recurring problem areas needing attention.

### 👩‍💼 Agent Performance
- Performance across agents is relatively consistent.
- Minor differences suggest opportunities for targeted training and performance optimization.

---

## 🚧 Challenges
- Inconsistent response time above target
- Persistent unanswered calls
- Moderate customer satisfaction levels
- Unresolved cases impacting service quality

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Improve staffing during peak call periods  
- Reduce response time to meet target benchmarks  
- Provide targeted training for agents  
- Address recurring issues (technical, payment, streaming)  
- Implement performance monitoring dashboards  
- Enhance customer experience strategies  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Excel  

---

## 🏁 Conclusion
The analysis reveals that while the call center demonstrates strong responsiveness and consistent agent performance, there are critical areas for improvement, particularly in response time, resolution rate, and customer satisfaction. Addressing these gaps will significantly enhance operational efficiency and service quality.
